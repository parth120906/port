SIMPLE OMELET RECIPE

Ah, the omelet! It's one of those wonderfully versatile dishes that can be as simple or as sophisticated as you like. At its core, an omelet is essentially a folded egg dish, but the variations are practically endless.

Prepration time

  • Total : approximately 10 minutes
  • Prepration: 5 minutes
  • Cooking : 5mintes

Ingredients

  • 2,3 large egg
  • Salt to taste
  • peppar to test
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional filling: cheese diced vegetable etc.

Instruction

  1. Crack eggs in a bowl, add the milk, and whisk until yolks break.
  2. Add milk, peppers, and onions and whisk again.
  3. Put bowl in microwave and cook for 1 minute, checking occasionally.
  4. Take out bowl, put omelet on a plate, sprinkle cheese and cook for 30 seconds to melt cheese.
  5. Take out and enjoy!
